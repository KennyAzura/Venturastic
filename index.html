Media and Entertainment

Market cap is set reach 2.4 trillion by 2024.

Global media and entertainment are a $2.1 trillion industry consisting of filmed entertainment (movies, television, digital video), audio (music, radio, podcasts), publishing (newspapers, magazines, digital publishers), and video games (console, PC, mobile, eSports.).


Media Industry has gone through a major transition, led by internet distribution and changing media consumption habits, this has opened up new opportunities, bringing in new winners.


From an investment perspective, it is of a distinctive nature, as it is a recession proof industry, due to its non-correlated to general economic trends, and people will consume media and entertainment content.

P&A Funding

Venturastic will organize financing for producers to finance marketing and distribution (“P&A”) costs so they will be able to hire distribution companies to distribute their motion pictures on favourable terms.

Print and Advertising costs, are expenses incurred by a theatrical distributor prior to and during the promotion and exhibition of feature motion pictures theatrically.


These costs include various forms of advertising including newspaper and television & radio ads as well as the cost of prints of the film that are projected in theatres. Venturastic will arrange financing for print and advertising costs for the theatrical release of feature motion pictures. Venturastic will evaluate completed motion pictures for such investments and also makes forward commitments for P&A at the time of production financing - in instances where Venturastic is financing production costs or is a part of other production financing arrangements. Forward commitments as this will enable producers to obtain higher presales and GAP financing as part of their production financing structure.

Corporate Social Responsibility

We sponsor Patient Capital and aim to back leaders from the marginalized communities who display determination, skills, imagination and understanding of what it takes to create change. Our program is designed to connect and cultivate a pipeline of entrepreneurs both Micro and Social who are committed to tackling poverty and injustice in their communities.

Patient capital is not a grant, it is an investment which is intended to recoup its principal. It would not seek to maximize financial returns on its investment, as we seek to maximize social impact, to capitalize the creation of markets to combat poverty and give an opportunity for growth to both Micro and Social enterprise.

With our patient capital program, we as the investor are willing to make a financial investment in a business with no expectation of turning a quick profit, instead investors will demand accountability in the form of return on capital and substantiated proof that the enterprise can grow sustainably in the long run. If we do have a very huge success story to share, we as a team would feel proud of being a part of that success.

Each year we aim to invest in 2 projects as a Patient Capital Investor and back these projects with our expertise, business advisory and strategic support, to ensure that they succeed and become self-sustainable.

We don’t just give the capital, but back the projects with our knowledge and experience.
